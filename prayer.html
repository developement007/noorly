Islamic Prayer Times

Prayer times are calculated based on your location using astronomical calculations. Fajr begins at dawn and ends at sunrise, Dhuhr starts after the sun passes the meridian, Asr begins when an object's shadow equals its height, Maghrib starts at sunset, and Isha begins when twilight ends.
